    Lists:

- item 1. 第一项前和最后一项后必须各留出一空行.
- item 2. List 由 "*", "+", "-" 等符号引导。

- item 3. 中间各项之间可留空行也可省略.

  1. nested item 1. 此前又须留出一空行。数字型的list接
     受这几种格式：

     a. 阿拉伯数字 1, 2, 3 ...
     #. 大写或小写字母 A, B, ..., Z. a, b, ..., z.
     #. 大写或小写罗马数字 I, II, ... 或 i, ii, ...

    Literal Blocks:

::

  Whitespace, newlines, blank lines, and all kinds of
  markup is preserved by literal blocks.

Per-line quoting can also be used on unindented
literal blocks (顶格写时每行的 ``>`` 符号不可少)::

> Useful for quotes from email and
> for Haskell literate programming.

Line Blocks: （注意渲染后的样式与普通文字一样）

| Line blocks are useful for addresses,
| verse, and adornment-free lists.

| Each new line begins with a
| vertical bar ("|").
|     Line breaks and initial indents
|     are preserved.
| Continuation lines are wrapped
  portions of long lines; they begin
  with spaces in place of vertical bars.

Doctest Blocks: 以 ``<<<`` 打头

>>> print "This is a doctest block."
This is a doctest block.
